Palette commits to warm ink, blood crimson, copper, and amber — film-lab heat, never cool gray or SaaS purple. 3. Motion is editorial choreography: scroll compresses the title like a reel leaving the gate; reveals land in sequence; grain and projector pulse keep the room alive. 4. Arabic is primary structure (dir=rtl, display face, hierarchy); French is secondary italic Latin with unicode-bidi isolation — not a mirrored afterthought. 5. Deliberately NOT: card grids of posters-as-SaaS, purple accents, stock hero photos, hamburger nav chrome, infinite-scroll catalogue energy, or CDN web fonts.
